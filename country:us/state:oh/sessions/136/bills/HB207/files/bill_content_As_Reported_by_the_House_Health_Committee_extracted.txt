Section 2:

Code be enacted to read as follows: 7

Wellness Month" to promote prostate exams, colonoscopies, and 9
mental health screenings among rural, urban, and suburban males. 10

promoting "Take Your Dad to the Doctor and Dentist Day" that 15
shall be publicly accessible for download on the department's 16

general health district or the authority having the duties of a 19
