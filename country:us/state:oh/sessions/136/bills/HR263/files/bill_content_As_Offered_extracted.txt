A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring Wendy Zhan on her retirement as the director
of the Ohio Legislative Service Commission.
WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th 1
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend Wendy Zhan on the 2
occasion of her retirement from the Ohio Legislative Service 3
Commission; and 4
WHEREAS, Throughout her distinguished tenure with the Ohio 5
Legislative Service Commission, Wendy Zhan has upheld the highest 6
standards of integrity and dedication. Commencing her career with LSC 7
as an intern, she joined the Legislative Budget Office as a budget 8
analyst with the education group in 1995 and went on to serve as the 9
division chief of the education group and the director of budget and 10
H. R. No. 263 Page 2

fiscal analysis before being named the tenth director of the 11
Legislative Service Commission in 2019. In the performance of her 12
duties, she has demonstrated the utmost efficiency and competence, and 13
she has displayed a genuine commitment to meeting the varying and 14
complex needs of the legislature; and 15
WHEREAS, The expertise that Wendy Zhan has shown, time and again, 16
has gained her the esteem of all those with whom she has worked, the 17
respect of legislators on both sides of the aisle, and the gratitude 18
of the public at-large. She has consistently brought the full measure 19
of her enthusiasm and intelligence to her roles, and she has executed 20
her responsibilities with a degree of precision and discretion that 21
exceeded all expectations; and 22
WHEREAS, In applauding Wendy Zhan for her steady leadership, we 23
note with pride that it is through the exemplary efforts of such 24
dedicated individuals that the Great State of Ohio remains responsive 25
to its citizens. It is our sincere hope that her sterling example of 26
analytical acumen and collaborative spirit will inspire future 27
generations to devote themselves similarly; therefore be it 28
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 29
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 30
extend special recognition to Wendy Zhan for her exceptional service 31
to the Ohio Legislative Service Commission and to the State of Ohio 32
and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further 33
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 34
a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Wendy Zhan. 35
