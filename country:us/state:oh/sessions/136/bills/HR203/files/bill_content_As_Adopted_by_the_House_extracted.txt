As Adopted by the House
136th General Assembly
Regular Session H. R. No. 203
2025-2026
Representative Santucci
A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring Saints Peter and Paul Byzantine Catholic
Church on its One Hundredth Anniversary.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Saints Peter and 2
Paul Byzantine Catholic Church on the auspicious occasion of its One 3
Hundredth Anniversary, commemorated June 29, 2025; and 4
WHEREAS, Recognition of this significant milestone is a fitting 5
tribute to Saints Peter and Paul Byzantine Catholic Church, for over 6
the past century, it has attained a praiseworthy record of service. 7
The achievements of this fine house of worship are a justifiable 8
source of pride and an excellent reflection not only on the church 9
itself but also on its devoted pastor, the Reverend Miron Kerul’-Kmec 10
Jr., on its members, and on the Warren community; and 11
WHEREAS, The source of many social, religious, and humanitarian 12
projects and programs, Saints Peter and Paul Byzantine Catholic Church 13
has enhanced the quality of life in the area since its inception in 14
1925, and its generous contributions have earned it the gratitude and 15
respect of the many citizens it has so capably served. We are certain 16
that in the years to come, the church will maintain the tradition of 17
spiritual enlightenment that has long been its hallmark; and 18
WHEREAS, The members of the Saints Peter and Paul Byzantine 19
Catholic Church have long been actively involved in, and concerned 20
about, the events in their community, and this celebration offers them 21
a unique opportunity to share their memories and to reflect on the 22
benefits of life in their close-knit community. The congregation can, 23
indeed, be proud of their church, for they have built on the best 24
traditions of the past to ensure a peaceful and robust future; 25
therefore be it 26
H. R. No. 203 Page 2
As Adopted by the House
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 27
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, we 28
commend Saints Peter and Paul Byzantine Catholic Church on its One 29
Hundredth Anniversary and extend best wishes for continued growth and 30
prosperity; and be it further 31
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 32
a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Saints Peter and Paul 33
Byzantine Catholic Church. 34
