As Adopted by the House
136th General Assembly
Regular Session H. R. No. 259
2025-2026
Representative Ray
A R E S O L U T I O N
WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th 1
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Wadsworth 2
Rotary Club on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundredth 3
Anniversary; and 4
WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is a fitting 5
tribute to the Wadsworth Rotary Club, for it has attained a remarkable 6
record of service to the area. Since its inception in 1925, this 7
association has exemplified the values of volunteerism, service, and 8
civic engagement, and its accomplishments are a justifiable source of 9
pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the group itself but 10
also on its dedicated members, both past and present, on the 11
community, and on its parent organization; and 12
WHEREAS, Over the past century, the Wadsworth Rotary Club has 13
enhanced the quality of life within the surrounding area, and through 14
its generous contributions, it has earned the respect and gratitude of 15
generations of people. We are certain that as this noteworthy 16
organization maintains its unfaltering commitment, it will continue in 17
the tradition of excellence that has long been its hallmark; and 18
WHEREAS, We are proud to note that it is through the unceasing 19
efforts of entities such as the Wadsworth Rotary Club that Ohio has 20
gained a reputation as a state that is responsive to the needs of its 21
citizens and has remained a pleasant place in which to live and work. 22
This fine group is truly deserving of high praise; therefore be it 23
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 24
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 25
commend the Wadsworth Rotary Club on its One Hundredth Anniversary and 26

extend best wishes for ongoing success; and be it further 27
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 28
a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Wadsworth Rotary 29
Club. 30
