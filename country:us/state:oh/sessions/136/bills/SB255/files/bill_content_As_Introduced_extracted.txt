Section 2:
Section 1. That section 175.50 of the Revised Code be 5
enacted to read as follows: 6
Sec. 175.50. (A) As used in this section : 7
(1) "Homeowner" means an individual who, jointly or 8
severally, has legal or equitable title to owner-occupied 9
housing located in Ohio together with the right to control or 10
possess that housing. "Homeowner" includes a purchaser of 11
housing pursuant to a land installment contract if that contract 12
vests possession and maintenance responsibilities in the 13
purchaser and a person who has care or control of housing as 14
executor, administrator, assignee, trustee, or guardian of the 15
estate of the owner of that housing. 16
(2) "Utility" means any of the following: 17
