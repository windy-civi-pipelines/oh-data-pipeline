2025-2026
Representative Manning
A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring Nathan Bright as a 2025 Division I State
track and field champion.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud Nathan Bright on 2
securing the 2025 Division I State Track and Field Championship title 3
in the high jump; and 4
WHEREAS, This accomplishment is truly noteworthy, for Nathan 5
Bright has clearly demonstrated his talent and perseverance, as well 6
as his mental and physical fortitude as an athlete. At this year’s 7
Division I State Track and Field Meet, he placed first in the high 8
jump with a leap of 6 feet, 9 inches, and he has exemplified all of 9
the characteristics that are essential for success, including courage, 10
determination, and an innate sense of the principles of good 11
sportsmanship; and 12
WHEREAS, In displaying such exceptional prowess as a senior member 13
of the North Ridgeville High School boys track and field team, Nathan 14
Bright has earned the appreciation of his fellow athletes, the 15
devotion of countless fans, and the respect of even his most 16
determined rivals. Competition in athletics has enabled him to learn 17
much about dedication and self-discipline, and we take this 18
opportunity to acknowledge his outstanding record of achievement; and 19
WHEREAS, Track and field requires exceptional physical talent and 20
natural ability, and Nathan Bright is to be commended for his 21
commitment to this demanding sport. In attaining his athletic goals, 22
he has consistently exhibited the skill and integrity that are the 23
hallmarks of a true champion, and he has a set a fine example for the 24
next generation of athletes at North Ridgeville High School; therefore 25
be it 26

As Offered
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 27
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 28
congratulate Nathan Bright on his tremendous performance at the 2025 29
Division I State Track and Field Meet and look to his future with 30
optimism; and be it further 31
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 32
a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Nathan Bright. 33
