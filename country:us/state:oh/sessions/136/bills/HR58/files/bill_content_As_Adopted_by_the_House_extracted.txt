As Adopted by the House
136th General Assembly
Regular Session H. R. No. 58
2025-2026
Representative McClain
A R E S O L U T I O N
Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary.
WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th 1
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend Bucyrus Copper Kettle 2
Works, Ltd., on the occasion of its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary; 3
and 4
WHEREAS, Bucyrus Copper Kettle Works is, indeed, deserving of high 5
praise, for throughout fifteen decades of operation, this fine company 6
has attained a noteworthy record of accomplishment. Indeed, this 7
enterprise has earned the gratitude and appreciation of many satisfied 8
customers, and its success is a justifiable source of pride and an 9
outstanding reflection not only on the business itself but also on its 10
owner, James Patrick, on its hard-working employees, and on the 11
Bucyrus community; and 12
WHEREAS, Since its establishment, Bucyrus Copper Kettle Works has 13
enhanced the quality of life within the surrounding area, and it has 14
consistently demonstrated an exemplary commitment to the highest 15
standards of workmanship. Indeed, it has gained a sterling reputation 16
for producing a variety of quality products, including kettles, hods, 17
decorative bowls, fire stands, tympani shells, and waste paper 18
baskets, and we have no doubt that as this worthy venture maintains 19
its dedication to service and achievement, it will continue to grow 20
and prosper; and 21
WHEREAS, Over the years, the proprietors of Bucyrus Copper Kettle 22
Works, both past and present, have epitomized America’s 23
entrepreneurial spirit, and it is through the contributions of 24
individuals such as these that our state has gained a reputation as a 25
friendly, productive, and pleasant place in which to live. We are 26

certain that in years to come, all those associated with this company 27
will uphold the tradition of excellence that has long been the 28
hallmark of the business; therefore be it 29
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 30
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 31
commend Bucyrus Copper Kettle Works, Ltd., on its One Hundred Fiftieth 32
Anniversary and extend best wishes for continued success; and be it 33
further 34
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 35
a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Bucyrus Copper Kettle 36
Works, Ltd. 37
