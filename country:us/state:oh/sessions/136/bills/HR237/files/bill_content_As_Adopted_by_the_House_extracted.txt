General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Centerville 2
High School Revengeful Mushrooms Destination Imagination team on 3
securing a share of first place in the technical challenge - breaking 4
point secondary level event at the 2025 Destination Imagination Global 5
Finals; and 6
WHEREAS, The members of the Centerville High School Revengeful 7
Mushrooms Destination Imagination team are, indeed, a remarkable group 8
of young academicians, successfully combining creativity and 9
innovation with collaborative effort and determination to establish 10
themselves as truly exemplary students. With commendable dedication, 11
they advanced to this year’s Global Finals in the technical challenge 12
- breaking point event, where they competed against other teams to 13
design and build assembly and destruction equipment to create and 14
dismantle a stack of items while also presenting a story, and due to 15
their tremendous performance, they tied for first place in the 16
secondary level; and 17
WHEREAS, In preparing themselves for this important contest, the 18
Centerville High School Revengeful Mushrooms Destination Imagination 19
team members have had the opportunity to develop and express their 20
individual abilities and to acquire knowledge and information which 21
will be of great benefit to them throughout their lives. Having worked 22
diligently to sharpen their skills, they confronted competition with 23
confidence and enthusiasm and distinguished themselves as outstanding 24
