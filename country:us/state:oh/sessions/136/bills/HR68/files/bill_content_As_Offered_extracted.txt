A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring Makennah Craft on securing the 2025 Girls
Wrestling State Championship in the 100-pound
weight class.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Makennah Craft 2
as a 2025 OHSAA State Wrestling Champion; and 3
WHEREAS, Displaying exceptional form, ability, leadership, and 4
dedication during the 2024-2025 season, Makennah Craft has certainly 5
distinguished herself as one of the most talented wrestlers in the 6
state. A senior member of the Jackson High School girls wrestling 7
team, she demonstrated her drive and skill by winning the state crown 8
this year, becoming only the fifth girl in state history to win four 9
state titles, and her outstanding 44-0 record this season caps a two- 10
year undefeated streak; and 11
WHEREAS, Throughout intense training and preparation, Makennah 12
Craft has consistently exhibited the talent, integrity, and motivation 13
that are the hallmarks of a true champion. Indeed, she has inspired 14
her teammates, thrilled her fans, gained the respect of her opponents, 15
and made her family, her coaches, and her community very proud; and 16
WHEREAS, Participation in wrestling has enabled Makennah Craft to 17
learn much about commitment, self-discipline, and leadership. 18
Commendably, she has revealed the skill, strength, and stamina that 19
befit a standout athlete, and it is no surprise that she has won so 20
many admirers. The remarkable skill and experience she has shown as a 21
wrestler will be of great benefit as she forges a bright, promising 22
future; therefore be it 23
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 24
