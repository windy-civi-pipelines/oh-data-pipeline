A R E S O L U T I O N
Division I State Championship in the pole vault.
WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th 1
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Claire Iaciofano 2
as the 2025 Ohio High School Athletic Association Division I State 3
Champion in the pole vault; and 4
WHEREAS, A junior at Turpin High School, Claire Iaciofano has 5
combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good 6
sportsmanship to establish herself as a truly exceptional athlete. 7
With admirable dedication, she has worked diligently toward attaining 8
ultimate proficiency in her sport, and as a result, she won first 9
place in the pole vault with a mark of 13 feet, 4 inches at this 10
year’s OHSAA Division I State Meet, her second consecutive state 11
title; and 12
WHEREAS, Claire Iaciofano’s accomplishments and leadership, both 13
in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and an 14
outstanding reflection not only on her but also on her family, her 15
teammates, her coaches, her school, and her community. Her unwavering 16
commitment to excellence has earned her the respect and esteem of 17
many, and she is certainly worthy of high praise; and 18
WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role 19
in the total education of our young people, we are overjoyed to pay 20
tribute to Claire Iaciofano on an exemplary season. Indeed, the 21
maturity and experience she has gained as a member of the Spartans 22
track and field team will aid her in meeting the challenges of the 23
future; therefore be it 24
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 25
