A R E S O L U T I O N
Ohio Senate.
WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly 1
of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Isaac Rogers for 2
his loyal service to the Ohio Senate; and 3
WHEREAS, Isaac Rogers is a remarkable individual, for he has 4
combined civic concern and dedication with selfless initiative to 5
become a dynamic member of the Ohio Senate staff. Commencing his 6
service in 2020, he has distinguished himself throughout his time as a 7
senior legislative aide to Senator Louis W. Blessing, and he has 8
brought tremendous passion to his important work. He has clearly shown 9
the potential of each person to have a positive effect on the quality 10
of life in our society; and 11
WHEREAS, Over the years, Isaac Rogers has given generously of his 12
time, energy, and abilities to assist the Ohio Senate and has 13
demonstrated a praiseworthy commitment to the success of state 14
government. He has played an important role in maintaining the 15
efficiency and effectiveness of the legislative process, and his 16
efforts have reinforced many services that are currently available to 17
the constituents of our state; and 18
WHEREAS, Isaac Rogers’ contributions to the Ohio Senate have been 19
greatly appreciated by all those who have had the pleasure of working 20
with him. He has displayed exceptional devotion, responsibility, and 21
enthusiasm in his endeavors, and we extend best wishes for health and 22
happiness in the years to come; therefore be it 23
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General 24
Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Isaac Rogers on 25
