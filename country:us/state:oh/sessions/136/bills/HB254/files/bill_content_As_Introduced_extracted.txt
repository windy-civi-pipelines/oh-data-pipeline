As Introduced
136th General Assembly
Regular Session H. B. No. 254
2025-2026
Representatives Baker, Hoops
To enact section 3701.147 of the Revised Code to 1
increase education, awareness, and understanding 2
of cognitive impairment, Alzheimer's disease, 3
and other types of dementia. 4

Section 2:
Section 1. That section 3701.147 of the Revised Code be 5
enacted to read as follows: 6
Sec. 3701.147. (A) The department of health, in 7
coordination with the department of aging, the commission on 8
minority health, and community organizations focusing on 9
Alzheimer's disease and other types of dementia awareness, shall 10
incorporate awareness information into relevant public health 11
outreach conducted by the department of health. 12
(B) The parties described in division (A) of this section 13
shall design the information to educate and increase awareness 14
and understanding among health care providers, community 15
organizations, and the general public on the following topics 16
related to cognitive impairment, Alzheimer's disease, and other 17
types of dementia: 18
(1) Early warning signs; 19
(2) The value of reducing risk factors of Alzheimer's 20
