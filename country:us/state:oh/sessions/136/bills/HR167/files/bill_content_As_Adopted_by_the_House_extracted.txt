As Adopted by the House
136th General Assembly
Regular Session H. R. No. 167
2025-2026
Representative Lorenz
A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring C.J. Sanna on winning a 2025 Division I
State Track and Field Championship.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud C.J. Sanna as the 2025 2
Ohio High School Athletic Association Division I State Track and Field 3
Champion in the boys discus; and 4
WHEREAS, C.J. Sanna’s outstanding ability and spirited drive, as 5
well as his quickness and tenacity, have distinguished him as a 6
superior athlete, and during the season, he has gained the respect of 7
all those who have witnessed him in action. A junior at Olentangy High 8
School, he has proven himself to be one of the finest athletes in 9
Ohio, as evidenced by his first-place throw of 194 feet, 11 inches in 10
the boys discus at this year’s Division I State Track and Field Meet; 11
and 12
WHEREAS, Few sports embody the spirit of individual accomplishment 13
more than track and field, and athletes such as C.J. Sanna exemplify 14
the qualities necessary for excellence in any sport, including 15
discipline, agility, conditioning, and focus. Indeed, his exceptional 16
prowess has won him numerous admirers; and 17
WHEREAS, In an educational system that seeks to teach the future 18
leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and 19
the rewards of individual effort, high school athletic competition has 20
become a valuable tool. Through his participation in sports, C.J. 21
Sanna has learned to meet challenges with enthusiasm and resolve; 22
therefore be it 23
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 24
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 25
H. R. No. 167 Page 2
As Adopted by the House
recognize C.J. Sanna on his outstanding achievement at the 2025 26
Division I State Track and Field Meet and offer best wishes for the 27
years ahead; and be it further 28
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 29
a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to C.J. Sanna. 30
