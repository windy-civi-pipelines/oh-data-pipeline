As Offered
136th General Assembly
Regular Session H. R. No. 135
2025-2026
Representative Deeter
A R E S O L U T I O N
Fiftieth Anniversary.
WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th 1
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize the Village of 2
LaGrange on the occasion of its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary; and 3
WHEREAS, Incorporated in 1875, LaGrange has become home to many 4
proud and conscientious individuals, and even as these fine people 5
have exuberantly embraced the promise of a bright future, they have 6
never relinquished their appreciation for the worthiest virtues of the 7
past. Without sacrificing their sense of tradition in the name of 8
progress, the people of LaGrange have managed to nurture a fresh 9
perspective and a positive outlook that endear this distinctive place 10
to all who know it well; and 11
WHEREAS, Throughout its existence, LaGrange has enjoyed the 12
benefits of a generous and civic-minded population, and those who 13
currently make the village their home take great pride in their 14
community and its rich heritage. Through their efforts, they have 15
certainly made the area a pleasant and friendly place in which to live 16
and work; and 17
WHEREAS, Every state depends upon such vibrant, durable 18
communities as LaGrange for its own survival and stability. Although 19
it has known adversity and tribulation, it also has been favored with 20
its share of blessings, and its historic buildings, solemn monuments, 21
and scenic public spaces have earned the respect and appreciation of 22
many thankful residents. In the years to come, the village is sure to 23
remain on its admirable course and to continue to transform challenges 24
into opportunities; therefore be it 25
H. R. No. 135 Page 2
As Offered
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 26
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 27
extend our congratulations to the Village of LaGrange as it 28
commemorates its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary and offer our best 29
wishes for its perennial good fortune; and be it further 30
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 31
a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Village of 32
LaGrange. 33
