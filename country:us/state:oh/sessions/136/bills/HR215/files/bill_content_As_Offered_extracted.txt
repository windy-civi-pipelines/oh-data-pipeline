A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring Marcus Blaze on winning a 2025 Division I
State Wrestling Championship.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud Marcus Blaze as the 2
2025 Division I State Wrestling Champion in the 150-pound weight 3
class; and 4
WHEREAS, This accomplishment is truly noteworthy, for Marcus Blaze 5
has clearly demonstrated his talent and perseverance, as well as his 6
mental and physical fortitude. As this year’s Division I State 7
Wrestling Champion in the 150-pound weight class, he holds the special 8
distinction of becoming one of our state’s few four-time champions, 9
and in winning this crown, he has exemplified all of the 10
characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of the 11
sports world, including courage, determination, and an innate sense of 12
the principles of good sportsmanship; and 13
WHEREAS, In displaying such outstanding prowess as a senior member 14
of the Perrysburg High School wrestling team, Marcus Blaze has earned 15
the appreciation of his fellow athletes, the devotion of countless 16
fans, and the respect of even his most determined rivals. Competition 17
in athletics has enabled him to learn much about dedication and 18
self-discipline, and we take this opportunity to acknowledge his 19
exemplary record of achievement; and 20
WHEREAS, Wrestling is a sport that requires exceptional strength, 21
stamina, and natural ability, and Marcus Blaze is to be commended for 22
his commitment to this demanding activity. In attaining his athletic 23
goals, he has consistently exhibited the skill and integrity that are 24
the hallmarks of a true champion; therefore be it 25
