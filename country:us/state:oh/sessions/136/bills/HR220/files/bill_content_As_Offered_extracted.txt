Honoring the Cincinnati Zoo and Botanical Garden on

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th 1
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Cincinnati 2
Zoo and Botanical Garden on its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary; and 3
WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is a fitting 4

beloved part of the community, and its many outstanding 9

working staff and volunteers; and 12
WHEREAS, Since its inception, the Cincinnati Zoo and Botanical 13
Garden has witnessed many changes, and it now stands as a testament to 14
the growth of its facilities and programs over the past century and a 15
half. We are certain that as this worthy endeavor maintains its 16
unfaltering dedication to service, it will continue in the tradition 17

of its visitors; and 19

the splendors and wonders of our natural world, and on this special 23
anniversary, they have accepted the challenge of carrying this 24
important and beloved work into the future; therefore be it 25
