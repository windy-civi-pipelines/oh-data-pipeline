To enact section 122.162 of the Revised Code to 1
create a food desert elimination grant program 2
and to make an appropriation. 3
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO:

Section 2:
Section 1. That section 122.162 of the Revised Code be 4
enacted to read as follows: 5
Sec. 122.162. (A) As used in this section, "food desert" 6
means a geographic area in which residents have limited access 7
to fruits, vegetables, and other healthful foods, through 8
grocery stores, farmer's markets, or other food providers, as 9
identified by the United States department of agriculture food 10
access research atlas. 11
(B) The department of development shall establish and 12
administer a food desert elimination grant program to 13
incentivize the establishment of grocery stores in food deserts 14
to provide access to healthy food in this state. The department 15
may adopt requirements and procedures as are necessary to 16
implement and administer the program. An individual grocery 17
retailer shall not receive a grant that exceeds fifteen thousand 18
dollars during a fiscal year. 19
