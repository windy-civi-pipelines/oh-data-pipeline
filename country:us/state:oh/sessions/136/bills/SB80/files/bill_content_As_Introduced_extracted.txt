Section 2:
Section 1. That section 1327.70 of the Revised Code be 4
amended to read as follows: 5
Sec. 1327.70. (A) As used in this section: 6
(1) "Biodiesel" and "blended biodiesel" have the same 7
meanings as in section 125.831 of the Revised Code. 8
"Conventional fuel" means gasoline and diesel fuel. 9
"Diesel fuel" has the same meaning as in section 5735.01 10
of the Revised Code. 11
(2) "Ethanol extended fuel" means a mixture of gasoline 12
and ethanol. 13
"Fuel inspector" means a county auditor or any person who 14
is designated by the county auditor to perform motor fuel 15
quality testing under this section. 16
