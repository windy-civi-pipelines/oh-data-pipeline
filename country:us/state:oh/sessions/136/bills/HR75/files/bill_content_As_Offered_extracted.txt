As Offered
136th General Assembly
Regular Session H. R. No. 75
2025-2026
Representative Ritter
A R E S O L U T I O N
29, 2025.
WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th 1
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize March 29, 2025, as 2
National Vietnam War Veterans Day; and 3
WHEREAS, The United States was involved in the Vietnam War for a 4
period of twenty years, and during that time, more than 8,744,000 5
Americans served in the armed forces, including more than 58,000 who 6
were killed, more than 300,000 who were wounded, and approximately 600 7
who were held as prisoners of war. On March 30, 1973, U.S. combat 8
troops were officially withdrawn from Vietnam, and in 2017, March 29 9
was designated National Vietnam War Veterans Day to honor of those who 10
fought, died, or are still unaccounted for as a result of the Vietnam 11
War; and 12
WHEREAS, This year marks fifty-two years after the end of 13
America’s role in the Vietnam War, and the 2025 National Vietnam War 14
Veterans Day underscores the importance of this period of history for 15
all Americans. It is especially important to commemorate the service 16
men and women who valiantly served our nation’s ideals and interests; 17
and 18
WHEREAS, It is imperative that we openly acknowledge our debt to 19
those who bore our country’s standard with courage, dignity, and 20
decency through their military service. It is, indeed, fitting that 21
National Vietnam War Veterans Day be observed to honor the selfless 22
and courageous members of the U.S. Armed Forces who demonstrated a 23
commendable adherence to the noble principles of democracy and love of 24
country through their outstanding military and community service 25
during the Vietnam War; therefore be it 26
H. R. No. 75 Page 2
As Offered
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 27
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 28
recognize March 29, 2025, as National Vietnam War Veterans Day; and be 29
it further 30
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 31
duly authenticated copies of this Resolution to the news media of 32
Ohio. 33
