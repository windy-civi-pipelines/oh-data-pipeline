2025-2026
Representative Klopfenstein
A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring Owen Scott on winning a 2025 Division II
State Track and Field Championship.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend Owen Scott as the 2025 2
Division II State Champion in the 1,600-meter run; and 3
WHEREAS, A diligent and capable individual, Owen Scott is 4
deserving of recognition for his exemplary accomplishments as a member 5
of the Van Wert High School track and field team. At the 2025 Division 6
II State Meet, he won first place in the 1,600-meter run with a 7
personal-best and school-record time of 4:10.19, and his notable 8
record of personal achievement stands as a hallmark for others; and 9
WHEREAS, Through his participation in sports, Owen Scott has 10
learned important lessons of discipline, perseverance, hard work, and 11
good sportsmanship that will undoubtedly be of great benefit to him 12
throughout his life. Through his outstanding efforts, he has inspired 13
many people to excel in various areas of endeavor, and he has set an 14
example worthy of emulation; and 15
WHEREAS, In a society that seeks to teach the future leaders of 16
our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards 17
of individual effort, athletic competition has become a valuable 18
educational tool. With sports contributing to the development of 19
countless youth, we are happy to congratulate such a fine athlete as 20
Owen Scott; therefore be it 21
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 22
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 23
applaud Owen Scott on winning the 2025 Division II State Championship 24
in the 1,600-meter run and extend best wishes for continued success; 25

and be it further 26
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 27
