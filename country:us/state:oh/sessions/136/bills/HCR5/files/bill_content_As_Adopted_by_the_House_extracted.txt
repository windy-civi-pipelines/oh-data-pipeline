message from the Governor as to the State of the State; and 12

adoption of the Resolution, call for a committee of nine on the part 14

Assembly to appoint a committee to wait upon the Governor; therefore 19
be it 20

Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, authorize a joint convention of the 22
