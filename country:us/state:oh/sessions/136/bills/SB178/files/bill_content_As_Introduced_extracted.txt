As Introduced
136th General Assembly
Regular Session S. B. No. 178
2025-2026
Senator Hicks-Hudson
Cosponsors: Senators Antonio, Craig, Smith
To enact section 2901.44 of the Revised Code to 1
create the Task Force on Missing Women and Girls 2
who are Black, Indigenous, or People of Color 3
and to make an appropriation. 4

Section 2:
Section 1. That section 2901.44 of the Revised Code be 5
enacted to read as follows: 6
Sec. 2901.44. (A) The task force on missing women and 7
girls who are Black, Indigenous, or People of Color is created. 8
The task force shall do all of the following: 9
(1) Work with state agencies to develop policy changes 10
that will address the lack of care and concern for missing and 11
murdered women and girls who are Black, Indigenous, or People of 12
Color and identify policies that will ensure that first 13
responders are culturally competent; 14
(2) Advance the knowledge of communities on the severity 15
of the issue of women and girls who are Black, Indigenous, or 16
People of Color who are missing and murdered; 17
(3) Ensure that communities of people who are Black, 18
Indigenous, or People of Color are educated and trained on 19

prevention and protection strategies and social media protocols 20
regarding women and girls who are Black, Indigenous, or People 21
of Color who are missing and murdered; 22
(4) Develop a strategy to collect statistics, 23
demographics, surveys, oral histories, and data analysis 24
relevant to women and girls who are Black, Indigenous, or People 25
of Color who are missing and murdered; 26
(5) Recommend preventive programming and ideas to advance 27
the safety of women and girls, including policies that address 28
the overlapping forms of oppression faced by women and girls who 29
are Black, Indigenous, or People of Color; 30
(6) Identify major traffic hubs, highways, and resource 31
extraction sites that lead to or are responsible for the 32
facilitation of the abduction of women and girls who are Black, 33
Indigenous, or People of Color; 34
(7) Create a statewide awareness campaign regarding women 35
and girls who are Black, Indigenous, or People of Color who are 36
missing and murdered; 37
(8) Hold public meetings throughout the state to solicit 38
public input; 39
(9) Solicit and accept written public input. 40
(B) The task force shall consist of the following members: 41
(1) The director of job and family services or the 42
director's designee; 43
(2) The executive director of the division of criminal 44
justice services or the executive director's designee; 45
(3) The superintendent of the state highway patrol or the 46
