To create the Ohio Food and Agriculture Policy 1
Council and to make an appropriation. 2

Section 2:
Section 1. (A) The Ohio Food and Agriculture Policy 3
Council is created to make recommendations to the General 4
Assembly regarding food systems that strengthen the state's food 5
and farm economies. The Council shall engage in advocacy, 6
education, and policy work to enhance the health and wellness of 7
the citizens of Ohio and to encourage the sustainability of 8
natural resources. 9
(B) The Council shall consist of the Director of 10
Agriculture or the Director's designee and all of the following 11
members appointed by the Governor: 12
(1) One member who represents the Ohio Hospital 13
Association; 14
(2) One member who represents Ohio State University 15
Extension; 16
(3) One member who represents Central State University 17
Extension; 18

(5) One member who represents urban farming; 20

(8) One member who is a registered lobbyist who represents 23
Ohio cooperatives. 24
