To enact section 3727.62 of the Revised Code 1
regarding the use of noncompete provisions in 2

contracts with nonprofit hospitals. 4

Section 2:
Section 1. That section 3727.62 of the Revised Code be 5
enacted to read as follows: 6
Sec. 3727.62. (A) As used in this section : 7
(1) "Advanced practice registered nurse" has the same 8
meaning as in section 4723.01 of the Revised Code. 9
(2) "Health care professional employee" means a physician, 10
physician assistant, or advanced practice registered nurse 11
employed by a nonprofit hospital. "Health care professional 12
employee" does not include a management employee employed by a 13
nonprofit hospital. 14
(3) "Management employee" means an employee who formulates 15
policy on behalf of a nonprofit hospital, who directs the 16
implementation of policy, or who may be reasonably required on 17
behalf of the hospital to have a major role in personnel 18
administration. 19

hospital that violates division (B) of this section is liable to 67
a health care professional employee or prospective health care 68
professional employee for damages and attorney's fees and costs. 69
