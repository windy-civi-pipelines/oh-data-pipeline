General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to 2
St. Paul Lutheran Church on its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary, 3
celebrated September 14, 2025; and 4
WHEREAS, Since its inception, St. Paul Lutheran Church has offered 5
its members the pathway to a more meaningful life by illuminating and 6
demonstrating the precepts on which it was founded. The spirit of love 7
and Christian fellowship that exists within the church also extends 8
beyond those sacred walls to touch the lives of everyone encountered 9
by these devout disciples; and 10
WHEREAS, Under the capable leadership of its pastors, both past 11
and present, St. Paul Lutheran Church has experienced continual growth 12
and has provided a source of moral guidance and spiritual motivation 13
for its congregation. Over the years, the church has enhanced the 14

has earned the gratitude and respect of the many citizens it has so 16
capably served; and 17

represents dedication and commitment. For a century and a half, the 19

the challenge of carrying their beliefs into the future; therefore be 22
it 23

the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 25
