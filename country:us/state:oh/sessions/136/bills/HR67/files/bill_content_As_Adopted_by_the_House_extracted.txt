136th General Assembly
Regular Session H. R. No. 67
2025-2026
Representatives Hiner, Lear
A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring Ashlynn Brokaw on winning a 2025 State
Wrestling Championship.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud Ashlynn Brokaw as the 2
2025 State Wrestling Champion in the 105-pound weight class; and 3
WHEREAS, This accomplishment is truly noteworthy, for Ashlynn 4
Brokaw has clearly demonstrated her talent and perseverance, as well 5
as her mental and physical fortitude. As this year’s State Champion in 6
the 105-pound weight class, she holds the special distinction of being 7
the first champion in program history, and in also winning the Central 8
District and sectional championships, she has exemplified all of the 9
characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of the 10
sports world, including courage, determination, and an innate sense of 11
the principles of good sportsmanship; and 12
WHEREAS, In displaying such outstanding prowess as a member of the 13
Mount Vernon High School wrestling team, Ashlynn Brokaw has earned the 14
appreciation of her fellow athletes, the devotion of countless fans, 15
and the respect of even her most determined rivals. Competition in 16
athletics has enabled her to learn much about dedication and self- 17
discipline, and we take this opportunity to acknowledge her exemplary 18
record of achievement; and 19
WHEREAS, Wrestling is a sport that requires exceptional strength, 20
stamina, and natural ability, and Ashlynn Brokaw is to be commended 21
for her commitment to this demanding activity. In attaining her 22
athletic goals, she has consistently exhibited the skill and integrity 23
that are the hallmarks of a true champion; therefore be it 24
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 25

the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 26
congratulate Ashlynn Brokaw on her personal success and salute her as 27

a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Ashlynn Brokaw. 30
