Honoring the Columbus Grove High School girls
basketball team as the 2025 Division VI State
Champion.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Columbus 2
Grove High School girls basketball team on winning the 2025 Division 3
VI State Championship; and 4
WHEREAS, A remarkable group of young athletes, the members of the 5
Columbus Grove High School girls basketball team have combined talent 6
and spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish 7
themselves as truly exceptional competitors. With admirable 8
dedication, the Bulldogs have worked diligently toward attaining 9
ultimate proficiency in basketball, and as a result, they defeated a 10
tough Rootstown team by the score of 65-46 to win this year’s Division 11
VI State Championship, their first state crown in program history, and 12
finished the season with an impressive 27-2 record; and 13
WHEREAS, The Bulldogs’ achievements and leadership are a 14
justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection on the 15
players, their families, their school, their community, and their 16
coach, Brian Schroeder. In leading his team to victory, Coach 17
Schroeder has not only helped the athletes to develop their talents to 18
the fullest but also instilled in them a valuable sense of 19
determination, perseverance, and camaraderie; and 20
WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role 21
in the total education of our young people, we are overjoyed to pay 22
tribute to these exemplary athletes and their outstanding coach for a 23
job well done. The maturity and experience the team members have 24
