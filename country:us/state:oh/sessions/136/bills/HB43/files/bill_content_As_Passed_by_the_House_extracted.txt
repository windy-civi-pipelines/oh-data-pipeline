Section 2:
Section 1. Due to winter weather for which the Governor 5
declared a state of emergency and the Ashtabula County Sheriff 6
declared multiple Level Three Snow Emergencies, and that caused 7
residential roads to be unusable for ten days, shutdown of all 8
essential services such as grocery stores and the local 9
hospital, local internet service to be inaccessible for up to a 10
month, structure and roof damage to the Ashtabula County 11
Emergency Response Facility and backup emergency response 12
facility, the collapse of Ashtabula Lakeside High School's roof, 13
and damage to the Ashtabula City School district administrative 14
offices and server which delayed the transition to remote 15
instruction, the Department of Education and Workforce shall do 16
both of the following for the 2024-2025 school year: 17
(A) Provide the Ashtabula City School District with 18

Section 2. This act is hereby declared to be an emergency 31
measure necessary for the immediate preservation of the public 32
peace, health, and safety. The reason for such necessity is to 33
