As Introduced
136th General Assembly
Regular Session H. B. No. 297
2025-2026
Representatives Ritter, Newman
To amend section 307.66 of the Revised Code to 1
increase the amount a county may provide to 2
military and veterans organizations for Memorial 3
Day expenses. 4

Section 2:
Section 1. That section 307.66 of the Revised Code be 5
amended to read as follows: 6
Sec. 307.66. The board of county commissioners, annually, 7
shall appropriate, and advance upon application made no more 8
than one hundred twenty days prior to memorial day, to each 9
garrison or naval branch of the Army and Navy Union, U.S.A. and 10
to each chartered post, garrison, or naval branch of any 11
organization of veterans recognized and chartered by the 12
congress, upon request of their officials, in the county, a sum 13
of money not to exceed five hundred one thousand dollars to aid 14
in defraying the expenses of memorial day. 15
Each such organization applying for an advance of funds 16
shall submit with its application a statement of expenses 17
incurred up to the time the application for an advance is 18
submitted. Each such organization receiving money from this 19
appropriation shall file a sworn statement accounting for the 20
