As Offered
136th General Assembly
Regular Session H. R. No. 6
2025-2026
Representative Manning
A R E S O L U T I O N
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF
OHIO:
WHEREAS, During the 136th General Assembly (2025-2026), 2
important conferences and meetings of national or statewide interest 3
may be called, especially of interest to the House of 4
Representatives; therefore be it 5
RESOLVED, By the members of the House of Representatives of the 6
136th General Assembly, that, if in the judgment of the Speaker, any 7
conferences or meetings, either national or state, are deemed to be 8
of sufficient importance to warrant attendance of members or officers 9
of the House, the Speaker shall designate members or officers to 10
attend; and be it further 11
RESOLVED, That expenses incurred incident thereto of any member 12
or officer so designated by the Speaker shall be paid out of 13
appropriate funds of the House. 14
