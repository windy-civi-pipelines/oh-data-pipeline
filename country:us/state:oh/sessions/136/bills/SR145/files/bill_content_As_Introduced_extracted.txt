136th General Assembly
Regular Session S. R. No. 145
2025-2026
Senator Landis
A R E S O L U T I O N
2025 Division III State Champion.
WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly 1
of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Dover High School softball 2
team on winning the 2025 Division III State Championship; and 3
WHEREAS, A remarkable group of young athletes, the members of the 4
Dover High School softball team have combined talent and spirit with 5
the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish themselves as 6
truly exceptional competitors. With admirable dedication, the Crimson 7
Tornadoes have worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency 8
in softball, and as a result, they defeated Holland Springfield by the 9
score of 10-0 to win this year’s Division III State Championship 10
title; and 11
WHEREAS, The Crimson Tornadoes’ achievements and leadership are a 12
justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection on the 13
players, their families, their school, their community, and their head 14
coach, Hannah Duff. In leading her team to victory, Coach Duff has not 15
only helped the athletes to develop their talents to the fullest but 16
also instilled in them a valuable sense of determination, 17
perseverance, and camaraderie; and 18
WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role 19
in the total education of our young people, we are overjoyed to pay 20
tribute to these exemplary athletes and their outstanding coach for a 21
job well done. The maturity and experience the team members have 22
gained through their participation in sports will greatly aid them in 23
meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it 24
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General 25
S. R. No. 145 Page 2

Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Dover High 26
School softball team on its tremendous season and salute the members 27
