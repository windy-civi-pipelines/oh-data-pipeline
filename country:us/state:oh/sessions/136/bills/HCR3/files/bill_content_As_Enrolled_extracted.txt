(House Concurrent Resolution Number 3)
A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Representatives authority to designate groups of members to prepare
arguments for and against amendments to the Ohio Constitution proposed by
the General Assembly; a person or persons to prepare an argument for any
law, section, or item submitted to the electors by referendum petition; and a
person or persons to prepare an argument against any constitutional
amendment or law proposed by initiative petition.
Be it resolved by the House of Representatives of the State of Ohio (the Senate concurring):
WHEREAS, When the General Assembly adopts a joint resolution proposing an amendment
to the Ohio Constitution under Ohio Constitution, Article XVI, Section 1, it may designate, under

Section 2:
section 3505.063 of the Revised Code, a group of members who voted in support of the joint
resolution to prepare arguments for the proposed amendment and, unless no members voted in
opposition, a group of members who voted in opposition to the joint resolution to prepare arguments
against the proposed amendment; and
WHEREAS, The General Assembly, if in session, is required under Ohio Constitution,
Article II, Section 1g and section 3519.03 of the Revised Code to name the person or persons who
will prepare the argument, explanation, or both, for any law, section, or item submitted to the electors
by referendum petition, or against any proposed constitutional amendment or law submitted by
initiative petition; now therefore be it
RESOLVED, That the President of the Senate and the Speaker of the House of
Representatives, on behalf of and during the 136th General Assembly, may designate a group of
members who voted in support of a joint resolution proposing an amendment to the Ohio
Constitution to prepare arguments for the proposed amendment and, unless no members voted in
opposition, a group of members who voted in opposition to the joint resolution to prepare arguments
against the proposed amendment; and be it further
RESOLVED, That the President of the Senate and the Speaker of the House of
Representatives, on behalf of and during the 136th General Assembly, may designate the person or
persons who will prepare the argument, explanation, or both, for any law, section, or item submitted
to the electors by referendum petition; and be it further
RESOLVED, That the President of the Senate and the Speaker of the House of
Representatives, on behalf of and during the 136th General Assembly, may designate the person or
persons who will prepare the argument against any constitutional amendment or law proposed by
initiative petition; and be it further
