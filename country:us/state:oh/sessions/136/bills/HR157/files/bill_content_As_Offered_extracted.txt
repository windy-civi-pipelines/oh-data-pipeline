Representative Manning
A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring Rania Brown as the 2025 Division I State
Discus Champion.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize Rania Brown, a 2
recent graduate of Avon High School, on winning the 2025 Division I 3
State Track and Field Championship in the discus; and 4
WHEREAS, Displaying exceptional form, ability, leadership, and 5
dedication during the 2025 season, Rania Brown certainly distinguished 6
herself as one of the most talented throwers in Ohio. After winning 7
the 2024 state championship in the discus, she once again prevailed in 8
this event at this year’s Division I State Meet with a mark of 153 9
feet, 4 inches, and she is deserving of the highest praise; and 10
WHEREAS, Throughout intense training and preparation, Rania Brown 11
has consistently demonstrated the talent, integrity, and motivation 12
that are the hallmarks of a true champion. She has inspired her 13
teammates, thrilled her fans, gained the respect of her opponents, and 14
made her coaches, family, and community proud; and 15
WHEREAS, In a society that seeks to teach the future leaders of 16
our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards 17
of individual effort, athletic competition has become a valuable 18
educational tool. Clearly, Rania Brown has learned much about 19
excellence as an athlete, and she has made a significant impact on 20
high school track and field in our state; therefore be it 21
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 22
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay 23
tribute to Rania Brown on her success and extend best wishes for the 24
years ahead; and be it further 25
H. R. No. 157 Page 2
As Offered
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 26
a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Rania Brown. 27
