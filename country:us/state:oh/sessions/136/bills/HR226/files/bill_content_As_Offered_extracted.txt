Representatives Ghanbari, Swearingen
A R E S O L U T I O N
College Football Hall of Fame.
WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th 1
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to 2
Urban Meyer on being inducted into the 2025 College Football Hall of 3
Fame; and 4
WHEREAS, As a college football coach, Urban Meyer has demonstrated 5
remarkable integrity and dedication. A former graduate assistant at 6
The Ohio State University and assistant at Illinois State, Colorado 7
State, and Notre Dame universities, he served as the head coach at 8
Bowling Green University, the University of Utah, the University of 9
Florida, and The Ohio State University during his two-decade career, 10
compiling an impressive 187-32 record and guiding his teams to seven 11
conference and three national championships, and he and many of his 12
players have won a variety of honors and awards. He went on to coach 13
at the professional level with the Jacksonville Jaguars for one 14
season, and he is currently a host and analyst on FOX’s Big Noon 15
Kickoff and a board member of several charitable organizations; and 16
WHEREAS, The expertise, guidance, and sound judgment of coaches 17
such as Urban Meyer are critical to the ultimate success of any 18
athletic endeavor. Due largely to his tireless efforts, his players 19
have emerged as winners, both in and out of athletic competition, and 20
he has gained the genuine appreciation and esteem of many for his 21
fantastic work; and 22
WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role 23
in the development of role models for our young people, we are 24
extremely pleased to pay tribute to Urban Meyer for a job well done. 25
His outstanding rapport with his players, as well as with his fellow 26

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 29
