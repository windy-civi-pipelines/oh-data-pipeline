As Adopted by the Senate
136th General Assembly
Regular Session S. R. No. 120
2025-2026
Senator Gavarone
A R E S O L U T I O N
WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly 1
of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Sandusky Lions Club on the 2
auspicious occasion of its One Hundredth Anniversary; and 3
WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is a fitting 4
tribute to the Sandusky Lions Club, for since its inception a century 5
ago, it has attained a remarkable record of service to the area. Since 6
it was chartered in 1925, this association has exemplified the values 7
of volunteerism, service, and civic engagement, and its 8
accomplishments are a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding 9
reflection not only on the group itself but also on its dedicated 10
members, both past and present, on the community, and on its parent 11
organization; and 12
WHEREAS, Over the past century, the Sandusky Lions Club has 13
enhanced the quality of life within the surrounding area, and through 14
its generous contributions, it has earned the respect and gratitude of 15
generations of people. We are certain that as this noteworthy 16
organization maintains its unfaltering commitment, it will continue in 17
the tradition of excellence that has long been its hallmark; and 18
WHEREAS, We are proud to note that it is through the unceasing 19
efforts of entities such as the Sandusky Lions Club that Ohio has 20
gained a reputation as a state that is responsive to the needs of its 21
citizens and has remained a pleasant place in which to live and work. 22
This fine group is truly deserving of high praise; therefore be it 23
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General 24
Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Sandusky 25
Lions Club on its One Hundredth Anniversary and extend best wishes for 26

ongoing success; and be it further 27
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly 28
authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Sandusky Lions Club. 29
