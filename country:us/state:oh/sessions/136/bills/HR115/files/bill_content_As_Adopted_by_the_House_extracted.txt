136th General Assembly
Regular Session H. R. No. 115
2025-2026
Representative Holmes
A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring the Maysville High School boys basketball
team as the 2025 Division IV State Champion.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Maysville 2
High School boys basketball team on winning the 2025 Division IV State 3
Championship; and 4
WHEREAS, A remarkable group of young athletes, the members of the 5
Maysville High School boys basketball team have combined talent and 6
spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish 7
themselves as truly exceptional competitors. With admirable 8
dedication, the Panthers have worked diligently toward attaining 9
ultimate proficiency in basketball, and as a result, they defeated a 10
tough Glenville team by the score of 74-67 to win this year’s Division 11
IV State Championship, the program’s first state crown; and 12
WHEREAS, The Panthers’ achievements and leadership are a 13
justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection on the 14
players, their families, their school, their community, and their 15
coaches. In leading their team to victory, the coaches have not only 16
helped the athletes to develop their talents to the fullest but also 17
instilled in them a valuable sense of determination, perseverance, and 18
camaraderie; and 19
WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role 20
in the total education of our young people, we are overjoyed to pay 21
tribute to these exemplary athletes and their outstanding coaches for 22
a job well done. The maturity and experience the team members have 23
gained through their participation in sports will greatly aid them in 24
meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it 25
H. R. No. 115 Page 2
As Adopted by the House
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 26
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 27
commend the Maysville High School boys basketball team on its 28
tremendous season and salute the members and coaches as some of Ohio’s 29
finest citizens; and be it further 30
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 31
a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Maysville High 32
School boys basketball team. 33
