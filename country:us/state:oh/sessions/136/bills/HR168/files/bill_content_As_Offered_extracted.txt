As Offered
136th General Assembly
Regular Session H. R. No. 168
2025-2026
Representatives Odioso, Abrams, Baker, Mathews, A.
A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring the St. Xavier High School boys lacrosse
team as the 2025 Division I State Champion.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud the St. Xavier High 2
School boys lacrosse team on placing first at the 2025 Division I 3
State Tournament; and 4
WHEREAS, Each member of the St. Xavier High School boys lacrosse 5
team is an exceptional young athlete, combining personal ability and 6
competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship. With 7
admirable dedication, the players have worked diligently toward 8
attaining ultimate proficiency in their sport, and as a result, the 9
Bombers secured this year’s Division I State Championship crown, the 10
fifth such title in program history; and 11
WHEREAS, The members of the St. Xavier High School boys lacrosse 12
team are to be commended, for their victories over a host of talented 13
opponents underscore the athletic prowess and indomitable spirit of 14
the players. Indeed, it is no surprise that their maturity and resolve 15
have gained them widespread respect, even among their most daunting 16
rivals; and 17
WHEREAS, Under the expert guidance of their coach, these gifted 18
athletes have diligently applied themselves to the quest for 19
excellence on the field, and time and again, they have risen to the 20
occasion, amply demonstrating their unflagging determination. 21
Participation in lacrosse has taught them a great deal about 22
commitment, self-discipline, and leadership, and clearly, their 23
accomplishments are deserving of recognition; therefore be it 24
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 25
H. R. No. 168 Page 2
As Offered
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 26
congratulate the St. Xavier High School boys lacrosse team on its 27
outstanding success this season and extend best wishes for the future; 28
and be it further 29
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 30
a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the St. Xavier High 31
School boys lacrosse team. 32
