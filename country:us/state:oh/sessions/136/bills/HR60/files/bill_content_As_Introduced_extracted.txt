2025-2026
Representatives Mathews, T., Santucci
A R E S O L U T I O N
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF
OHIO:
WHEREAS, Ohio is the birthplace of aviation and has 2
produced some of our nation's greatest pioneers in space 3
exploration, including more astronauts than any other state; and 4
WHEREAS, Ohio continues to lead the nation in aerospace 5
research and development and is home to world-class research 6
institutions such as NASA Glenn Research Center, NASA Neil 7
Armstrong Test Facility, and the Air Force Research Laboratory; 8
and 9
WHEREAS, Ohio's highly skilled workforce, robust 10
transportation infrastructure, and developed manufacturing 11
sector make Ohio a strategic location to support NASA's 12
operations; and 13
WHEREAS, Relocating NASA headquarters to Cleveland would 14
honor Ohio's contributions to aerospace and space exploration 15
and best position NASA for sustained success; therefore be it 16
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of 17
Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, strongly 18
encourage President Trump, Vice President Vance, and NASA 19
Administrator Designate Isaacman to relocate NASA headquarters 20

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives 22
