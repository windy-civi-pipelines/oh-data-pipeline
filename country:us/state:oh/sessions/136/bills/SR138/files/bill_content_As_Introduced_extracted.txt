A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring Harold Bozeman for his service to the State
of Ohio.
WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly 1
of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Harold Bozeman on the memorable 2
occasion of his retirement from the Ohio Legislative Service 3
Commission, June 30, 2025; and 4
WHEREAS, Harold Bozeman’s sterling reputation and dedication to 5
public service have been a source of pride throughout the whole of his 6
thirty-eight-year tenure with our state. Commencing his career with 7
the Ohio House of Representatives in 1987, he served as a trusted 8
sergeant-at-arms for Ohio’s iconic speakers Vern Riffe and JoAnn 9
Davidson, and he continued to set himself apart as a consummate 10
professional and an asset to our state’s legislative endeavors when he 11
joined the Ohio Legislative Service Commission in 2000. Indeed, he has 12
been celebrated for being an earnest supporter of Ohio and its elected 13
officials; and 14
WHEREAS, Harold Bozeman has been one of the Ohio Legislative 15
Service Commission’s most capable ambassadors, and by giving 16
graciously of his time, skill, and expertise, he has demonstrated a 17
praiseworthy commitment to the success of the agency as well as to the 18
effectiveness of the Ohio General Assembly; and 19

WHEREAS, Harold Bozeman’s expansive professional endeavors have 20
been instrumental to the Ohio Legislative Service Commission, to the 21
Ohio General Assembly, and to the public-at-large, but most of all, he 22
has been greatly admired by his colleagues and all those who have had 23
the pleasure of working with him. Throughout his distinguished career, 24
he has embodied the tenets of dependability, sincerity, and altruism 25
that have benefited his peers and his fellow citizens, and his 26
exemplary work ethic has become his enduring legacy; therefore be it 27
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General 28
Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, extend a heartfelt 29
salute to Harold Bozeman, a fine Ohioan, and offer best wishes for a 30
happy, healthy retirement; and be it further 31
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly 32
authenticated copy of this Resolution to Harold Bozeman. 33
