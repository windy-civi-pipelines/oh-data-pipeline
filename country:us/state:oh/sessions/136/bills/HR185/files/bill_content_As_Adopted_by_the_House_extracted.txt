Representative John
A R E S O L U T I O N
Anniversary.
WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th 1
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to 2
the Mansfield Playhouse on its Centennial; and 3
WHEREAS, This significant milestone is a fine time to acknowledge 4
the Mansfield Playhouse, for this troupe has achieved a fine 5
reputation in the area since its establishment a century ago as the 6
Community Players. A creative and talented group of individuals, the 7
members of the company have demonstrated exemplary initiative, 8
enthusiasm, and perseverance in developing and sharing their skills in 9
preparation for each of their performances, and their unwavering 10
commitment to excellence has undoubtedly earned them a reputation as 11
gifted and competent artists; and 12
WHEREAS, The second oldest continuously-producing community 13
theater, as well as the largest producing company, in Ohio, the 14
Mansfield Playhouse has experienced tremendous prosperity and become a 15
beloved organization for area residents. Clearly, the troupe’s 16
dedication to the best interests of its patrons has been evident in 17
all of its endeavors; and 18
WHEREAS, In this time of increased emphasis on industry, science, 19
and technology, it is reassuring to note that our nation’s strong 20
artistic heritage is being perpetuated and advanced through the 21
efforts of concerned individuals such as the members of the Mansfield 22
Playhouse. All those who have given of their time, energy, and 23
resources to bring the company’s productions to fruition have clearly 24
shown the potential of individuals to have a positive effect on life 25
in our society, and we applaud them on their outstanding work; 26
H. R. No. 185 Page 2
As Adopted by the House
therefore be it 27
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 28
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 29
congratulate the Mansfield Playhouse as it celebrates its One 30
Hundredth Anniversary and salute its members and staff as some of 31
Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further 32
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 33
a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Mansfield 34
Playhouse. 35
