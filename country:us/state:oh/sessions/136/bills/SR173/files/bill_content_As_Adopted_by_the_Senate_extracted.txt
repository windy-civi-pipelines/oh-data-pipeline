WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly 1

WHEREAS, The talented Anthony Wayne High School baseball team is, 4
indeed, to be heartily commended for decisively earning the 2025 5
Division II State Championship title, the first in program history, 6

performance reflects the outstanding collective effort by a gifted 8
group of athletes, and without exception, the team has devoted 9

dividends; and 12
WHEREAS, The members of the Anthony Wayne High School baseball 13
team have epitomized the power, poise, and precision that befit true 14
champions. Despite sometimes grueling pressure, they have consistently 15
maintained their composure and morale, exhibiting strength, speed, 16
stamina, and agility in every challenging encounter. Time and again, 17
they have risen to the occasion, amply demonstrating their unflagging 18
determination, and their impressive maturity and finesse have earned 19
them widespread respect, even among their most formidable rivals; and 20
WHEREAS, By weathering a rigorous schedule and prevailing over a 21
host of adversaries, the members of the Anthony Wayne High School 22
baseball team have learned much about dedication, self-discipline, 23
leadership, and perseverance. Their first-place finish is a measure of 24
their stalwart commitment to the sport, and their indomitable spirit 25
has made their families, friends, and coach incredibly proud; 26
