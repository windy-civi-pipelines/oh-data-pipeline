Representative Hoops
A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring Arianna Kiessling on securing a 2025
Division II State Championship in girls bowling.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to 2
Arianna Kiessling as the 2025 Division II State Champion in girls 3
bowling; and 4
WHEREAS, Arianna Kiessling is deserving of this prestigious honor, 5
a tribute to the remarkable athletic gifts and indomitable spirit that 6
she has so amply displayed as a junior member of the Napoleon High 7
School girls bowling team. A tenacious competitor, she decisively 8
secured this year’s 2025 Division II State Championship crown with a 9
total score of 703, and it is fitting that such an achievement be 10
enthusiastically commemorated; and 11
WHEREAS, Arianna Kiessling has conscientiously applied herself to 12
the quest for excellence as an athlete, training and practicing 13
diligently in order to ensure peak conditioning, and her efforts have 14
been an inspiration to her peers and coaches. Indeed, she can always 15
be counted on not only to shine individually but also to lend her 16
support to her fellow team members, and understandably, the talents 17
and skills that she has exhibited have earned her many admirers; and 18
WHEREAS, Time and again, Arianna Kiessling has risen to the 19
occasion, maintaining her morale, composure, and good sportsmanship, 20
and she has gained the respect of even her most determined rivals. 21
Clearly, her enthusiastic involvement in bowling has enabled her to 22
learn much about dedication, self-discipline, and leadership, and in 23
the process, her exceptional prowess has made her family and her 24
friends very proud; therefore be it 25

As Offered
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 26
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay 27
tribute to Arianna Kiessling on winning the 2025 Division II State 28
Championship title and extend best wishes for the future; and be it 29
further 30
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 31
a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Arianna Kiessling. 32
