A R E S O L U T I O N
Hundredth Anniversary.
WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th 1
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Sidney First 2
Presbyterian Church on its Bicentennial; and 3
WHEREAS, Since its inception, Sidney First Presbyterian Church has 4
offered its members the pathway to a more meaningful life by 5
illuminating and demonstrating the precepts on which the church was 6
founded. The spirit of love and Christian fellowship that exists among 7
the parishioners not only prevails within the church edifice itself 8
but also extends beyond those sacred walls to touch the lives of 9
everyone encountered by these devout disciples; and 10
WHEREAS, Under the capable leadership of its pastors, Sidney First 11
Presbyterian Church has experienced continual growth and has provided 12
a source of moral guidance and spiritual motivation for each of its 13
members. Over the years, the church has enhanced the quality of life 14
within the surrounding area, and through its generous efforts, it has 15
earned the gratitude and respect of the many citizens it has so 16
capably served; and 17
WHEREAS, An occasion such as this is an important milestone. For 18
two hundred years, the members and leadership of Sidney First 19
Presbyterian Church have remained ever faithful, and on this special 20
anniversary, they have accepted the challenge of carrying their 21
beliefs into the future; therefore be it 22
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 23
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 24
commend Sidney First Presbyterian Church on its Two Hundredth 25
