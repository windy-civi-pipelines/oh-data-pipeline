As Adopted by the House
136th General Assembly
Regular Session H. R. No. 175
2025-2026
Representative Deeter
A R E S O L U T I O N
Track and Field Champion.
WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th 1
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Ariannah Floyd on 2
winning the 2025 Division II State Championship in the 300-meter 3
hurdles; and 4
WHEREAS, A senior at Firelands High School, Ariannah Floyd has 5
combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good 6
sportsmanship to establish herself as a truly exceptional young 7
athlete. With admirable dedication, she has worked diligently toward 8
attaining ultimate proficiency in track and field, and as a result, 9
she placed first in the 300-meter hurdles with a time of 42.76 seconds 10
at this year’s Division II State Meet; and 11
WHEREAS, Ariannah Floyd’s accomplishments and leadership, both in 12
and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and a fine 13
reflection not only on her but also on her family, her teammates, her 14
coaches, her school, and her community. Her unwavering commitment to 15
excellence has earned her the respect and esteem of many, and she is 16
certainly worthy of high praise; and 17
WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role 18
in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased 19
to pay tribute to Ariannah Floyd on an exemplary season. Indeed, the 20
same maturity and experience she has gained through her participation 21
in track and field will greatly aid her in meeting the challenges of 22
the future; therefore be it 23
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 24
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 25
H. R. No. 175 Page 2
As Adopted by the House
commend Ariannah Floyd as the 2025 Division II State Track and Field 26
Champion in the 300-meter hurdles and extend best wishes for continued 27
success; and be it further 28
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 29
a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Ariannah Floyd. 30
