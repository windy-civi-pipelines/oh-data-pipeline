General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Westlake United 2
Methodist Church on its Two Hundredth Anniversary, celebrated 3
September 6-7, 2025; and 4
WHEREAS, Recognition of this significant milestone is a fitting 5
tribute to Westlake United Methodist Church, for over the past two 6
centuries, it has attained a praiseworthy record of service. Since its 7
founding in 1825 as Dover Methodist Episcopal Church, Westlake UMC has 8
thrived and flourished, and the achievements of this fine house of 9
worship are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection 10
not only on the church itself but also on its devoted leaders and 11
members and on Westlake as a whole; and 12
WHEREAS, The source of numerous social, religious, and 13
humanitarian projects and programs, Westlake United Methodist Church 14
has improved the area, and its tremendous contributions have gained it 15
the esteem and appreciation of the entire community. We are certain 16
that in the years to come, the church will maintain the tradition of 17
spiritual enlightenment that has long been its hallmark; and 18
WHEREAS, A church anniversary is a special event that reinforces 19
the enduring faith of the congregation and its leaders. Indeed, all 20
those associated with Westlake United Methodist Church have given 21

initiatives, and they are truly worthy of acknowledgement; therefore 23
be it 24
