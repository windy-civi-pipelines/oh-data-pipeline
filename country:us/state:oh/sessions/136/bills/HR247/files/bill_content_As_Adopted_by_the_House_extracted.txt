136th General Assembly

2025-2026
Representatives Miller, M., John
A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring Herlihy Funeral Home on its Centennial.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Herlihy Funeral 2
Home on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundredth Anniversary; and 3
WHEREAS, Recognition of this distinguished event is a fitting 4
tribute to Herlihy Funeral Home for its lasting presence in Mansfield 5
and the surrounding community throughout the past century. Established 6
by Joseph Geiger in 1925, this fine venture is now operated by a 7
fourth generation of the owners, and it has served area families with 8
unwavering commitment, respect, and integrity; and 9
WHEREAS, The success of Herlihy Funeral Home is a justifiable 10
source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the business 11
itself but also on its astute management and hard-working employees. 12
This respected establishment has helped countless people cope with the 13
challenges of losing a loved one by offering support, comfort, and 14
dignified ceremonies; and 15
WHEREAS, Over the years, Herlihy Funeral Home has assisted 16
individuals and families during bereavement, and in doing so, it has 17
earned a well-deserved reputation for compassion and dependability. We 18
are certain that as this noteworthy enterprise maintains an 19
unfaltering dedication to service and achievement, it will continue to 20
prosper and will uphold the tradition of excellence that has long been 21
its hallmark; therefore be it 22
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 23
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 24
commend Herlihy Funeral Home on its One Hundredth Anniversary and 25
extend best wishes for the future; and be it further 26
H. R. No. 247 Page 2
As Adopted by the House
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 27
a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Herlihy Funeral Home. 28
