Representative Piccolantonio
A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring the New Albany High School boys doubles
tennis team on winning the 2025 Division I State
Championship.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend the New Albany High 2
School boys doubles tennis team as the 2025 Division I State Champion; 3
and 4
WHEREAS, The members of the New Albany High School boys doubles 5
tennis team have proven to be a talented and superbly conditioned duo, 6
and Jackson Clements and Abhinav Dandu have gained the respect of fans 7
and opponents alike for their spirited and highly motivated 8
performance. Throughout the season, each player worked diligently to 9
improve his skills on the court, and as a result, they accomplished 10
their goals, overcoming a tough pair from Sylvania Southview High 11
School by a score of 6-4, 6-4 to bring home this year’s Division I 12
State Championship; and 13
WHEREAS, Known as a driven and determined doubles tennis team, 14
Jackson Clements and Abhinav Dandu have developed into a remarkably 15
efficient pair whose skill is equaled only by their exemplary display 16
of teamwork. They are a credit not only to their families and their 17
school but also to the superb leadership of their coaches; and 18
WHEREAS, The road to victory is a difficult one, requiring 19
ability, endurance, and a positive attitude on the part of players and 20
coaches alike. The members of the New Albany High School boys doubles 21
tennis team have exemplified the characteristics that are essential 22
for success both in and out of competition, including courage, 23
discipline, integrity, and good sportsmanship, and their efforts have 24
