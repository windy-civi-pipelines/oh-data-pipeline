responsible for licensing child care centers, type A family 8
child care homes, and type B family child care homes. Each 9
entity operating a head start program shall meet the criteria 10
for, and be licensed as, a child care center. The director is 11
responsible for the enforcement of this chapter and of rules 12
promulgated pursuant to this chapter. 13
