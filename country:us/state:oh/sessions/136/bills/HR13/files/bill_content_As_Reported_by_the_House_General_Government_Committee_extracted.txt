A R E S O L U T I O N
To recognize the sovereignty of the Republic of Cyprus 1
over the entirety of the island, and to memorialize 2
the 50th anniversary of the Turkish invasion and 3
occupation of the northern part of the island. 4
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF
OHIO:
WHEREAS, On July 20, 1974, Turkish troops invaded the 5
island of Cyprus and captured and occupied the northern part of 6
the island; and 7
WHEREAS, The Turkish invasion ended in the partition of 8
Cyprus along the UN-monitored Green Line, which still divides 9
Cyprus; and 10
WHEREAS, United Nations Security Council Resolution 360, 11
adopted on August 16, 1974, declared the Council's respect for 12
the sovereignty, independence, and territorial integrity of the 13
Republic of Cyprus, and formally recorded its disapproval of the 14
unilateral military actions taken against it by Turkey; and 15
WHEREAS, In 1983, the Turkish Cypriot assembly declared 16
independence of the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus. 17
Immediately upon this declaration, Britain convened a meeting of 18
the United Nations Security Council to condemn the declaration 19
as "legally invalid." United Nations Security Council Resolution 20
