A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring Meadow Tian as the 2025 Division I State
Champion in girls golf.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Meadow Tian on 2
winning the 2025 Division I State Championship in girls golf; and 3
WHEREAS, A senior member of the Olentangy High School girls golf 4
team, Meadow Tian has combined talent and competitive spirit with the 5
highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish herself as a truly 6
exceptional young athlete. With admirable dedication, she has worked 7
diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency on the links, and as 8
a result, she posted an impressive 139 to win this year’s Division I 9
State Championship in girls golf; and 10
WHEREAS, Meadow Tian’s accomplishments and leadership, both in and 11
out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and a fine 12
reflection not only on her but also on her family, her teammates, her 13
coach, Micah Conley, and the whole of the Braves school community. Her 14
unwavering commitment to excellence has earned her the respect and 15
esteem of many, and she is certainly worthy of high praise; and 16
WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role 17
in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased 18
to pay tribute to Meadow Tian on an exemplary season. Indeed, the 19
maturity and experience she has gained through her participation in 20
golf will greatly aid her in meeting the challenges of the future; 21
therefore be it 22
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 23
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 24
commend Meadow Tian on securing the 2025 Division I State Championship 25
