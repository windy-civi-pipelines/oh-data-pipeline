As Adopted by the House
136th General Assembly
Regular Session H. R. No. 236
2025-2026
Representative White, A.
A R E S O L U T I O N
Wrestling Champion.
WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th 1
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Bronson Begley on 2
winning the 2025 Division II State Wrestling Championship in the 3
126-pound weight class; and 4
WHEREAS, A student at Alter High School, Bronson Begley has 5
combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good 6
sportsmanship to establish himself as a truly exceptional young 7
athlete. With admirable dedication, he has worked diligently toward 8
attaining ultimate proficiency in wrestling, and as a result, he 9
defeated a tough opponent from Galion High School by the score of 4-1 10
to win this year’s Division II State Championship in the 126-pound 11
weight class; and 12
WHEREAS, Bronson Begley’s accomplishments and leadership, both in 13
and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and a fine 14
reflection not only on him but also on his family, his teammates, his 15
coaches, his school, and his community. His unwavering commitment to 16
excellence has earned him the respect and esteem of many, and he is 17
certainly worthy of high praise; and 18
WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role 19
in the total education of our young people, we are overjoyed to pay 20
tribute to Bronson Begley on an exemplary season. Indeed, the same 21
maturity and experience he has gained through his participation in 22
wrestling will greatly aid him in meeting the challenges of the 23
future; therefore be it 24
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 25

commend Bronson Begley as a 2025 Division II State Wrestling Champion 27

a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Bronson Begley. 30
