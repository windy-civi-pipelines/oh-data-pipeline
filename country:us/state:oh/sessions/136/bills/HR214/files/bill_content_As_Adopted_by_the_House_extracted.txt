A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring Grey Burnett as a 2025 Division I State
Wrestling Champion.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Grey Burnett on 2
winning the 2025 Division I State Wrestling Championship in the 120- 3
pound weight class; and 4
WHEREAS, A student at Perrysburg High School, Grey Burnett has 5
combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good 6
sportsmanship to establish himself as a truly exceptional young 7
athlete. With admirable dedication, he has worked diligently toward 8
attaining ultimate proficiency in wrestling, and as a result, he 9
defeated a tough opponent from Perry High School by the score of 5-1 10
to win this year’s Division I State Championship in the 120-pound 11
weight class, his second state title; and 12
WHEREAS, Grey Burnett’s accomplishments and leadership, both in 13
and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and a fine 14
reflection not only on him but also on his family, his teammates, his 15
coaches, his school, and his community. His unwavering commitment to 16
excellence has earned him the respect and esteem of many, and he is 17
certainly worthy of high praise; and 18
WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role 19
in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased 20
to pay tribute to Grey Burnett on an exemplary season. Indeed, the 21
same maturity and experience he has gained through his participation 22
in wrestling will greatly aid him in meeting the challenges of the 23
future; therefore be it 24
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 25
