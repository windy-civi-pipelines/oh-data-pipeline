WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is a fitting 5
tribute to Order of the Eastern Star Jewel Chapter 20, for since its 6
inception one hundred thirty years ago, it has attained a remarkable 7
record of service to the area. Under the jurisdiction of the Amaranth 8
Grand Chapter of the Order of the Eastern Star-Prince Hall Affiliated, 9
this association provides opportunities to support a variety of worthy 10
causes, and its accomplishments are a justifiable source of pride and 11
an outstanding reflection not only on the group itself but also on its 12
dedicated members, both past and present, and on the Toledo community; 13
and 14
WHEREAS, Over the past thirteen decades, Order of the Eastern Star 15
Jewel Chapter 20 has enhanced the quality of life within the 16
surrounding area, and through its generous contributions, it has 17
earned the respect and gratitude of generations of people. We are 18
certain that as this noteworthy organization maintains its unfaltering 19
commitment, it will continue in the tradition of excellence that has 20
long been its hallmark; and 21
WHEREAS, We are proud to note that it is through the unceasing 22
efforts of entities such as Order of the Eastern Star Jewel Chapter 20 23
that Ohio has gained a reputation as a state that is responsive to the 24
needs of its citizens and has remained a pleasant place in which to 25
live and work. This fine group is truly deserving of high praise; 26
