136th General Assembly
Regular Session H. R. No. 93
2025-2026
Representative Young
A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring the Centerville High School coeds varsity
dance team as the 2025 OASSA Jazz State Champion.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to 2
the Centerville High School coeds varsity dance team on winning the 3
Jazz Championship at the 2025 Ohio Association of Secondary School 4
Administrators State Dance Championships; and 5
WHEREAS, The talented athletes of the Centerville High School 6
coeds varsity dance team are, indeed, to be commended for their 7
remarkable fitness and competitive drive, qualities that have figured 8
prominently in the squad’s impressive performance at this year’s OASSA 9
State Dance Championships, including placing first in the jazz 10
category, second in the pom category, and third in the hip hop 11
category. To their credit, the members could always be counted on to 12
execute even the most demanding routines with agility, precision, 13
exuberance, and grace; and 14
WHEREAS, Under the expert guidance of their coaches, the members 15
of the Centerville High School coeds varsity dance team have trained 16
tirelessly in order to ensure peak physical and mental conditioning, 17
and their exceptional prowess has earned them many admirers. 18
Participation in this sport has enabled them to learn much about 19
creative expression, discipline, and dedication, and clearly, the 20
experience they have acquired is certain to benefit them in the years 21
to come; and 22
WHEREAS, Their enthusiastic spirit and love of sports has led all 23
those who comprise the Centerville High School coeds varsity dance 24
team to take part in many activities, and they have served as an 25
inspiration to their peers. Their accomplishments are a justifiable 26
