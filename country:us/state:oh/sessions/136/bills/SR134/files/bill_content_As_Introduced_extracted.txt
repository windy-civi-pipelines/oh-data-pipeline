WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly 1

WHEREAS, Hunter Spangler’s outstanding ability and spirited drive, 4
as well as his quickness and tenacity, have distinguished him as a 5
superior athlete and an exemplary member of the Liberty Center High 6
School track and field team. At this year’s Division III State Meet, 7
he placed first in the shot put with a throw of 63-1.25 feet, and he 8
has gained the respect and admiration of all those who have witnessed 9
him in action; and 10
WHEREAS, Few events embody the spirit of individual accomplishment 11
more than shot put. Athletes such as Hunter Spangler exemplify the 12
qualities necessary for excellence in any sport, including discipline, 13
conditioning, and focus. Indeed, throughout long hours of practice and 14
competition, he has consistently demonstrated the talent and 15
motivation that are the hallmarks of a true competitor; and 16
WHEREAS, Through his participation in sports, Hunter Spangler has 17
learned lessons of perseverance, hard work, and fair and honest 18
competition that will undoubtedly be of great benefit to him 19
throughout his lifetime. Time and again, he has risen to the occasion, 20
maintaining his morale, composure, and good sportsmanship, and he has 21
set a fine example that is sure to inspire future generations; 22
therefore be it 23
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General 24
Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Hunter 25
