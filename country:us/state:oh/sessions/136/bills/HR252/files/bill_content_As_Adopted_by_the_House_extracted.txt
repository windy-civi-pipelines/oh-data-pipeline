As Adopted by the House
136th General Assembly
Regular Session H. R. No. 252
2025-2026
Representative Ghanbari
A R E S O L U T I O N
Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary.
WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th 1
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to 2
Trinity United Methodist Church on the hallowed occasion of its One 3
Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary; and 4
WHEREAS, Since its inception, Trinity United Methodist Church has 5
offered its members the pathway to a more meaningful life by 6
illuminating and demonstrating the precepts on which the church was 7
founded. The spirit of love and Christian fellowship that exists among 8
the parishioners not only prevails within the church edifice itself 9
but also extends beyond those sacred walls to touch the lives of 10
everyone encountered by these devout disciples; and 11
WHEREAS, Under the capable leadership of the church’s pastors, 12
both past and present, Trinity United Methodist Church has experienced 13
continual growth and has provided a source of moral guidance and 14
spiritual motivation for each of its members. Over the years, the 15
church has enhanced the quality of life within the surrounding area, 16
and through its generous efforts, it has earned the gratitude and 17
respect of the many citizens it has so capably served; and 18
WHEREAS, An occasion such as this is an important milestone that 19
represents dedication, commitment, and enduring faith. For one hundred 20
fifty years, the members of Trinity United Methodist Church have kept 21
their faith alive in the community, and on this special anniversary, 22
the congregation has accepted the challenge of carrying its beliefs 23
into the future; therefore be it 24
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 25
H. R. No. 252 Page 2
As Adopted by the House
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 26
congratulate Trinity United Methodist Church on its One Hundred 27
Fiftieth Anniversary and pay tribute to all those joining in the 28
celebration of this joyous event; and be it further 29
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 30
a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Trinity United 31
Methodist Church. 32
