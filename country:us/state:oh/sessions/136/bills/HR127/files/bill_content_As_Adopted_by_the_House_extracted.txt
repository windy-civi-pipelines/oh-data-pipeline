As Adopted by the House
136th General Assembly
Regular Session H. R. No. 127
2025-2026
Representative Upchurch
A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring the Great Lakes Science Center on the
Centennial of the SS William G. Mather.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Great Lakes 2
Science Center on the One Hundredth Anniversary of the construction of 3
the SS William G. Mather, commemorated May 16, 2025; and 4
WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is a fitting 5
tribute to the Great Lakes Science Center, for it has attained a 6
remarkable record of service to its patrons by preserving and 7
celebrating the area’s rich history, including that of the SS William 8
G. Mather as well as its namesake’s legacy of innovation and impact on 9
Cleveland’s manufacturing industry. Indeed, the science center has 10
nurtured the steamship into becoming a beloved part of the community, 11
and its many outstanding accomplishments are a justifiable source of 12
pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the organization but 13
also on the hard-working staff and volunteers; and 14
WHEREAS, The Great Lakes Science Center has stood as a testament 15
to the community’s growth over the past century, and it has made 16
sincere investments in bringing to life the golden era of steamships 17
and Cleveland’s historical maritime significance through the restored 18
Great Lakes freighter, the SS William G. Mather. We are certain that 19
as this worthy institution maintains its unfaltering dedication to 20
service, it will continue in the tradition of excellence that has 21
become its hallmark and has enriched the lives of its visitors; and 22
WHEREAS, This special anniversary for the SS William G. Mather is 23
an important milestone that represents dedication and commitment. We 24
applaud the stewards of the Great Lakes Science Center for preserving 25
unique pieces that represent the community’s past, and salute them for 26

accepting the challenge of carrying this history into the future; 27
therefore be it 28
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 29
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 30
commend the Great Lakes Science Center on the Centennial of the 31
construction of the SS William G. Mather and look to its future with 32
optimism; and be it further 33
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 34
a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Great Lakes 35
Science Center. 36
