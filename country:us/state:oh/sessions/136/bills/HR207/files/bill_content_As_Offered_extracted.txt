A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring Crane Township on its Bicentennial.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Crane Township 2
on its Bicentennial, celebrated August 16, 2025; and 3
WHEREAS, Over the past two hundred years, Crane Township has 4
experienced dramatic growth and change, and yet, commendably, it still 5
radiates vitality, optimism, and a charm all its own. Established in 6
1825 and named in honor of Oliver Crane, the township has adapted to 7
meet each new challenge, and clearly, the community’s outstanding 8
schools, remarkable businesses, and astute civic organizations both 9
embody and thrive upon the values of which our flag is an emblem; and 10
WHEREAS, Even as they have exuberantly embraced the promise of a 11
bright future, the residents of Crane Township have never relinquished 12
their appreciation for the worthiest virtues of the past. Indeed, they 13
have managed to nurture a fresh perspective and a positive outlook 14
that continue to endear this distinctive place to all who know it 15
well; and 16
WHEREAS, Every state depends upon such vibrant, durable 17
communities as Crane Township for its own survival and stability. 18
Although having known adversity and tribulation, it also has been 19
favored with its share of blessings, and in the years to come, the 20
municipality is sure to remain on its admirable course and to continue 21
to transform challenges into opportunities; therefore be it 22
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 23
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 24
congratulate Crane Township as it commemorates its Two Hundredth 25
Anniversary and offer our best wishes for its perennial good fortune; 26

and be it further 27
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 28
a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Crane Township. 29
