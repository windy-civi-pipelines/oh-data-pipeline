2025-2026
Representative White, A.
A R E S O L U T I O N
State Track and Field Championship.
WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th 1
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend Logan Wilkins as the 2
2025 Division I State Champion in the boys 110-meter hurdles; and 3
WHEREAS, A diligent and capable individual, Logan Wilkins is 4
deserving of recognition for his exemplary accomplishments as a member 5
of the Fairmont High School track and field team. At the 2025 Division 6
I State Meet, he won first place in the boys 110-meter hurdles with a 7
time of 13.88 seconds, and his notable record of personal achievement 8
stands as a hallmark for others; and 9
WHEREAS, Through his participation in sports, Logan Wilkins has 10
learned important lessons of discipline, perseverance, hard work, and 11
good sportsmanship that will undoubtedly be of great benefit to him 12
throughout his life. Through his outstanding efforts, he has inspired 13
many people to excel in various areas of endeavor, and he has set an 14
example worthy of emulation; and 15
WHEREAS, In a society that seeks to teach the future leaders of 16
our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards 17
of individual effort, athletic competition has become a valuable 18
educational tool. With sports contributing to the development of 19
countless youth, we are delighted to congratulate such a fine athlete 20
as Logan Wilkins; therefore be it 21
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 22
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 23
applaud Logan Wilkins on winning a Division I State Championship and 24
extend best wishes for continued success; and be it further 25
