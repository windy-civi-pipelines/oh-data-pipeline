As Introduced
136th General Assembly
Regular Session S. R. No. 102
2025-2026
Senators Roegner, Weinstein
A R E S O L U T I O N
National Coach of the Year Award.
WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly 1
of Ohio are pleased to commend John Groce as the 2025 Hugh Durham 2
National Coach of the Year; and 3
WHEREAS, John Groce is truly worthy of recognition, for as the 4
head coach of The University of Akron men’s basketball team, he has 5
completed an array of challenging duties with genuine distinction, 6
proving himself to be a tireless champion of athletic excellence. This 7
season, he guided his squad to a program best 28-7 record, including 8
17-1 in Mid-American Conference play, the MAC Tournament championship 9
for a second consecutive year, and the program’s seventh appearance in 10
the NCAA Tournament. Truly, he has inspired trust and confidence by 11
motivating students and coaches alike to set ambitious goals and high 12
standards for themselves; and 13
WHEREAS, This notable accomplishment is a justifiable source of 14
pride and an excellent reflection on John Groce but also on his 15
family, his players, his school, and his community. Without question, 16
he has been one of The University of Akron’s most precious assets, and 17
his exceptional record of personal and professional achievement stands 18
as a hallmark worthy of emulation; and 19
WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role 20
in the total education of our young people, we are overjoyed to pay 21
tribute to Coach Groce and his fine players for a job well done. The 22
maturity and experience the team members have gained under his 23
mentorship will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the 24
future; therefore be it 25
S. R. No. 102 Page 2
As Introduced
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General 26
Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to John 27
Groce as the 2025 Hugh Durham National Coach of the Year and offer 28
best wishes for continued success; and be it further 29
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly 30
authenticated copy of this Resolution to John Groce. 31
