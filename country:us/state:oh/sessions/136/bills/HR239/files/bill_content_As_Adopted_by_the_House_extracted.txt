III State Track and Field Championship.
WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th 1
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to 2
Beau Hesler as the 2025 Ohio High School Athletic Association Division 3
III State Champion in the 800-meter run; and 4
WHEREAS, This accomplishment is truly noteworthy, for Beau Hesler 5
has clearly demonstrated his talent and perseverance, as well as his 6
mental and physical fortitude as a runner. As this year’s Division III 7
State Champion in the 800-meter run with a personal-best and school- 8
record time of 1:51.78, he has exemplified all of the characteristics 9
that are essential for success both in and out of the sports world, 10
including courage, determination, and an innate sense of the 11
principles of good sportsmanship; and 12
WHEREAS, In displaying such outstanding prowess as a member of the 13
North Adams High School track and field team, as well as the first 14
state champion in the history of the school, Beau Hesler has earned 15
the appreciation of his fellow athletes, the devotion of countless 16
fans, and the respect of even his most determined rivals. We take this 17
opportunity to acknowledge his exemplary record of achievement; and 18
WHEREAS, Running is an event that requires exceptional stamina and 19
natural ability, and Beau Hesler is to be commended for his commitment 20
to this demanding activity. In striving to attain his athletic goals, 21
he has consistently exhibited the skill and integrity that are the 22
hallmarks of a true champion; therefore be it 23
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 24
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 25
