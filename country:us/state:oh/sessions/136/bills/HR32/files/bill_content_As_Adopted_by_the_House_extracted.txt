unfaltering devotion, she has been selected as this year’s Ohio Fairs’ 13
Queen; and 14

unquenchable enthusiasm will be of great value to her in the years to 18
come; and 19
WHEREAS, The achievements of Amberly Szczerbiak are a justifiable 20
