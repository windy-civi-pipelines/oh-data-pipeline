136th General Assembly

Representative Swearingen
A R E S O L U T I O N
Anniversary.
WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th 1
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to 2
the Sandusky Lions Club on its Centennial; and 3
WHEREAS, Over the years, the Sandusky Lions Club has consistently 4
displayed volunteerism, community service, and civic engagement. 5
Chartered on July 1, 1925, it is one of only two Lions clubs worldwide 6
to be sponsored directly by the International Association of Lions 7
Clubs rather than another local club, and it has striven to support 8
individuals with vision needs and to build a stronger, more inclusive 9
community through its various activities and projects; and 10
WHEREAS, Due to the tremendous effort and initiative it has 11
exhibited, the Sandusky Lions Club has grown significantly during the 12
last century, and this noteworthy organization is certainly deserving 13
of acknowledgement. This special anniversary offers a unique 14
opportunity to reflect on the group’s tradition of success and to look 15
with optimism to the future; and 16
WHEREAS, It is not surprising that the Sandusky Lions Club has 17
achieved widespread recognition, for it has far surpassed the 18
expectations of its founders. Indeed, it exemplifies the essence of 19
the American spirit of generosity and service and sets the standard to 20
which other such organizations can aspire; therefore be it 21
RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of 22
the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, 23
congratulate the Sandusky Lions Club on the occasion of its Centennial 24
and salute its members as some of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it 25
H. R. No. 145 Page 2
As Offered
further 26
RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit 27
a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Sandusky Lions 28
Club. 29
