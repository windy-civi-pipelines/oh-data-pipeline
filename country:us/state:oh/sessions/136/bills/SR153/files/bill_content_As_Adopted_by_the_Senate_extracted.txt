A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring Ricardo DeLaSerda, Jr. on his retirement
from Ohio Legislative Information Systems.
WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly 1
of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Ricardo DeLaSerda, Jr., on 2
retiring from Ohio Legislative Information Systems; and 3
WHEREAS, Rick DeLaSerda is a truly remarkable individual, for he 4
has combined civic concern and diligence with selfless initiative to 5
become an invaluable asset to Ohio Legislative Information Systems. 6
Joining the agency in 2000, he has brought to LIS a wealth of 7
experience gained as a computer engineer with Texas Instruments for 8
twenty-four years. Indeed, he has performed his duties and fulfilled 9
his responsibilities with the utmost efficiency and competence across 10
his two and a half decades of distinguished and outstanding public 11
service; and 12
WHEREAS, Throughout his career, Rick DeLaSerda has given 13
generously of his time, energy, and abilities to assist the Ohio 14
General Assembly, and he has displayed a noteworthy dedication to the 15
success of state government. He has provided a valuable service by 16
helping to maintain the efficiency and effectiveness of the 17
legislative process, and he is deserving of high praise; and 18
WHEREAS, We are proud to note that it is through the unsurpassed 19
