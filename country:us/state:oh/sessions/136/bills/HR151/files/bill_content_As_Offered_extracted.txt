A R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring the Olentangy Liberty High School girls
lacrosse team as the 2025 Division I State
Champion.
General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud the Olentangy Liberty 2
High School girls lacrosse team on placing first at the 2025 Division 3
I State Championship; and 4
WHEREAS, Each member of the Olentangy Liberty High School girls 5
lacrosse team is an exceptional young athlete, combining personal 6
ability and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good 7
sportsmanship. With admirable dedication, the players have worked 8
diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in their sport, and 9
as a result, the Olentangy Liberty Patriots secured this year’s 10
Division I State Championship crown in double overtime by defeating a 11
strong Upper Arlington team by the score of 9-8, earning their second 12
consecutive such title; and 13
WHEREAS, The members of the Olentangy Liberty High School girls 14
lacrosse team are to be commended, for their victories over a host of 15
talented opponents underscore the athletic prowess and indomitable 16
spirit of each player. Indeed, it is no surprise that their maturity 17
and resolve have gained them widespread respect, even among their most 18
daunting rivals; and 19
WHEREAS, Under the expert guidance of their coach, these gifted 20
athletes have diligently applied themselves to the quest for 21
excellence on the field, and time and again, they have risen to the 22
occasion, amply demonstrating their unflagging determination. 23
Participation in lacrosse has taught them a great deal about 24
