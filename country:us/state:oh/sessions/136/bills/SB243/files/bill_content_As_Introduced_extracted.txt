As Introduced
136th General Assembly
Regular Session S. B. No. 243
2025-2026
Senators Weinstein, DeMora
Cosponsors: Senators Antonio, Craig, Ingram, Smith
To enact section 4501.41 of the Revised Code to 1
require the Registrar of Motor Vehicles and 2
deputy registrars to assist customers with 3
locating and claiming unclaimed funds. 4

Section 2:
Section 1. That section 4501.41 of the Revised Code be 5
enacted to read as follows: 6
Sec. 4501.41. (A) The registrar of motor vehicles or a 7
deputy registrar shall ask each person conducting a transaction 8
in person at the bureau of motor vehicles or at a deputy 9
registrar office if the person desires the registrar or deputy 10
registrar to check if the person has unclaimed funds under 11
Chapter 169. of the Revised Code. If the person requests the 12
registrar or deputy registrar to check for unclaimed funds, the 13
registrar or deputy registrar shall do so using the person's 14
name and any other relevant information available. 15
(B) The registrar or deputy registrar that conducts a 16
check under division (A) of this section shall inform the person 17
whether or not the person may have unclaimed funds and, if so, 18
ask whether the person would like assistance submitting a claim 19

like assistance, the registrar or deputy registrar shall assist 21
